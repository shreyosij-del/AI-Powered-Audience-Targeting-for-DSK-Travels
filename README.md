Target Audience Demographics

Adventure Travelers (Ages 22-35): Backpackers and explorers seeking thrilling experiences in Dubai’s desert and urban landscapes.

Family Tourists (Ages 28-50): Parents and vacation planners looking for memorable, tailored experiences with loved ones.

Business Travelers (Ages 30-60): Professionals navigating Dubai’s corporate landscape who require efficient, premium services.

Luxury & Honeymoon Seekers: High-spending leisure seekers and newlyweds looking for exclusive, romantic, and premium offers.

AI Targeting & Segmentation Strategy
The project employs advanced behavior-based and geographic targeting to reach the right users:


Behavioral Signals: Identifying international travelers, frequent flyer loyalty members, and online shoppers actively searching for travel deals.
+1


Seed Interests: Focusing on users interested in major attractions like Dubai Tourism and premium services like Emirates Airline.


Geographic Focus: Primary targeting regions include India and the United Kingdom, with secondary focus on GCC countries and Africa.

Lookalike Funnel & Retargeting
To ensure a continuous flow of customers, the strategy utilizes a "Lookalike Funnel":


1% Converters: Targeting a small, high-value group that most closely mirrors existing successful customers.


2-5% Engagers: Re-engaging potential customers who have interacted with social media posts or watched brand videos.
+2


5-10% Expansion: Broadening reach to broader, "lookalike" audiences to fuel the top of the funnel.


Retargeting: Connecting with past website visitors and WhatsApp leads through interactive Stories and direct messaging.

Expected Outcomes
By implementing this AI-powered structure, the project aims to achieve:

A 30% increase in Click-Through Rates (CTR).

A 20% reduction in Cost Per Lead (CPL).

An additional 50+ monthly inquiries.
